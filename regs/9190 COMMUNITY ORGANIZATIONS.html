District Regulation

9190 - COMMUNITY ORGANIZATIONS

Section: Community Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009

 

The Superintendent directs each Building Principal to:

 

1. Establish and maintain a file of community resources.

 

2. Recommend which instructional program and/or district operations would profit by the involvement of community resources personnel.

 

 

Issued:  20 October 2009

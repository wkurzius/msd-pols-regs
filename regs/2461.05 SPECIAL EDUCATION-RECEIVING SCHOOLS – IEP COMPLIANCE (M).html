The programs and services provided by the receiving school will be in accordance with the requirements of N.J.A.C. 6A:14-1.1 et seq.

 

A. Procedures To Ensure The Provision Of Services Required By The Individualized Education Program (IEP)

 

 

1. If a change in the delivery of special education or related services is necessary due to a change in personnel or pupil need, the receiving school will contact the sending district to convene an IEP team meeting to review and, if appropriate, revise the IEP.

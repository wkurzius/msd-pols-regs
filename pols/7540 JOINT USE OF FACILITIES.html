District Policy

7540 - JOINT USE OF FACILITIES

Section: Property Date Created: May 2022 Date Edited: May 2022

The Board of Education supports the joint expenditure of school district funds and municipal or county funds to provide facilities from which the community may derive benefits.

 

The Board may, as the opportunity or need arises, join with

 

 

_X__   the local municipal governing body

 

_X_     each or all of the governing bodies of municipalities comprising the school district

 

_X_     the Board of County Commissioners of the county

 

in acquiring, improving, equipping, operating, or maintaining jointly used facilities in accordance with applicable law.

 

 

N.J.S.A. 18A:20-19; 18A:20-34

District Policy

8130 - SCHOOL ORGANIZATION (M)

Section: Operations Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009

 

M

 

The Board of Education directs the organization of the instructional program of this district in the following schools and grades:

 

Schools        Grades

 

Weston School     Kindergarten - 3

Roosevelt Elementary School        4 – 5

Alexander Batcho Intermediate     6 – 8

School          

Manville High School    9 - 12

 

The Superintendent shall continually monitor the effectiveness of the district organizational plan and recommend to the Board such modifications in the plan as may be in the best interest of pupils, make most effective use of district resources, and serve the educational goals of the Board.

 

 

Adopted:  20 October 2009

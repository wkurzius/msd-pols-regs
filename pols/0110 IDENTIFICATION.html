District Policy

0110 - IDENTIFICATION

Section: Bylaws Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: February 2023

 

Name

 

The official name of the Board of Education shall be “The Board of Education of Manville in the County of Somerset.”

 

Purpose

 

The Board of Education exists for the purpose of providing a thorough and efficient system of free public education in grades Pre-Kindergarten through twelve in the Manville School District.

 

Composition

 

The Manville School District is comprised of all the area within the municipal boundaries of Manville in the County of Somerset.

 

Classification

 

The school district shall be classified as a Type II district.

 

Address

 

The address of the Board of Education shall be:

 

1100 Brooks Boulevard

Manville, New Jersey  08835

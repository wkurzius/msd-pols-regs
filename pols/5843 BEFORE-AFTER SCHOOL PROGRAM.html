District Policy

5843 - BEFORE/AFTER SCHOOL PROGRAM

Section: Students Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009

 

A Before/After School Program is offered to pupils who attend school at Weston.  The Before School Program starts at 7:00 a.m.  The After School Programs runs from 2:35 â€“ 6:00 p.m.  On half days the After School Program will start at 12:10 p.m.

 

A sliding scale based on family size and income is used to determine fees.  Family and Community Services of Catholic Charities with the approval of the Manville Board of Education sponsors the program.

 

Weston School staff are not part of the program.  The district supports the program by providing them with relevant attendance information regarding the pupils who are enrolled in the program.  The school district has no influence as to who gets into the program.

 

 

Adopted:  20 October 2009

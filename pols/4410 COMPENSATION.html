District Policy

4410 - COMPENSATION

Section: Support Staff Date Created: October 2009 Date Edited: October 2009

 

The Board of Education will establish the compensation for support staff members not covered by the terms of a negotiated agreement or in an individual contract with the Board.

 

 

N.J.S.A. 18A:6-6; 18A:16-11

 

 

Adopted:  20 October 2009
